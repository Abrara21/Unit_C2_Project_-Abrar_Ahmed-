9. Common Mistakes to Avoid

Divs and Spans

<div> and <span> are non-semantic elements. They have no meaning by themselves and should be used sparingly, only when no other semantic element is appropriate.

Use semantic elements like <header>, <section>, and <footer> instead, as they convey meaning about the content.

Correct Use of Elements

Understanding when to use semantic elements is important:

  • <article>: Represents a standalone topic or piece of content (e.g., a blog post, news article).
  • <section>: A thematic division of content, often part of a larger topic.
